Careers at The Obvious

Join Our Team

We're building something different — a news organization focused on clarity, independence, and impact. If you're passionate about quality journalism and want to help cut through the noise, we'd love to hear from you.

Open Positions

We're currently a lean team, but we're always interested in hearing from talented people. We're particularly interested in:

  • Writers & Reporters — with expertise in politics, technology, markets, or international affairs
  • Editors — who can sharpen prose and maintain our voice
  • Data Journalists — who can find stories in numbers
  • Product & Engineering — to build great reader experiences

What We Value

  • Intellectual curiosity and rigor
  • Clear, direct communication
  • Independence and integrity
  • A healthy skepticism of conventional wisdom
  • The ability to explain complex topics simply

Get in Touch

There's no formal application process. If you think you'd be a good fit, send us an email telling us who you are and what you'd bring to The Obvious.
